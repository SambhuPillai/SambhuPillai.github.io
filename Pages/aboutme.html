About Me

Hello fellow readers! My name is Sam, and this webiste is dedicated to me and my journey...

Education

I undertook a bachelors degree in Mathematics at the University of Reading. After three years of study in this course, I achieved a first class and straight away I decided to further my understanding in this subject by doing a masters. In 2016, I went to University College London to undertake a masters degree in Mathematical Modelling. Both universities provided different experiences for me, and if I was to compare both of them, I will definitely say Reading was by far the better university.

Work Experience

After I finished my masters degree, I decided that it is time for me to get some work experience. I joined a company called StreetPR, which was essentially being a brand ambassador for many brands, such as gyms, lesuire centers and stuff. I did this role mainly so I can start making some money as well as to get experience for future opportunities. I also did a bit of tutoring for A-level and GCSE Maths, which was something a did enjoy on the weekends. In 2018, I joined The Language Shop as a Service Coordinator. My role involved finding interpreters to cover bookings that were made by clients for their patients.

Other

One of my hobbies is I like tutoring now and then. It's something I like to do on the weekends. It keeps my maths skills fresh in my head and it acts as a stress relief for the hard labours I do during the week.
